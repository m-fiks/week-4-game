Crystal Collector!

Click the button to start the game!

When the game begins, a random number will show up. It is your job to try and match your score to this number by clicking the crystals.

Each crystal will add a random value to your score. Click wisely and try to remember the value each crystal holds once you find out what it is!

If your score goes over the value of the random number, you lose. :(

If your score matches the random number, you win! :)
